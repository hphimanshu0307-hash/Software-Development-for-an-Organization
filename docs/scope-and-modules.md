# System Scope & Modules

This document outlines the functional scope of the ERP system.

---

## Functional Scope

- Inspections and operations management
- Personnel records and role management
- Compliance documentation and audits
- Financial tracking and reporting
- Document management
- Analytics and performance monitoring

---

## System Philosophy

The system was designed to reduce fragmentation, improve reliability, and
support informed decision-making across the organization.
